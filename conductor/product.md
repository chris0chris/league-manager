# Initial Concept
LeagueSphere is a sophisticated flag football league management system designed to handle complex tournament scheduling, player eligibility (passcheck), live scoring, and league standings.

## Target Users
- **League and Team Managers:** Primary users for administrative features including league setup, team registration, and complex tournament scheduling.
- **Players and Fans:** Primary users of the Liveticker, viewing real-time game updates, schedules, and league standings.
- **Gameday Officials:** Field users responsible for player verification (Passcheck) and real-time score entry (Scorecard).

## Core Goals
- **Streamlined Operations:** Digitalize gameday processes (Passcheck, Scorecard) to improve reliability and speed.
- **Simplified Management:** Reduce the administrative burden of managing league and team schedules through automation and intuitive tools.
- **Complex Scheduling:** Provide a robust, hierarchical Gameday Designer to handle intricate tournament formats and playoff progressions, supported by a real-time validation system.
- **Centralized Source of Truth:** maintain an automated and accurate record of player eligibility, game results, and league standings.
- **Enhanced Fan Experience:** Deliver real-time gameday updates and accessible league data to players and fans.

## Key Features
- **Gameday Creation & Designer:** A hierarchical tool (Field > Stage > Game) for creating gamedays. Features include template-based tournament generation with flexible progression mapping, automatic time calculation with manual overrides, integrated team pool management, and an interactive validation system with real-time error highlighting.
- **Passcheck (Eligibility Verification):** A digital system to enforce complex eligibility rules and manage player lists for gamedays.
- **Liveticker & Scorecard:** Real-time game monitoring and efficient, mobile-friendly scoring tools for officials.
- **Automated Standings Calculation:** Real-time calculation and presentation of league tables based on verified game results.

## User Experience (UX)
- **Functional & Robust:** Prioritize data integrity and system reliability for critical gameday operations.
- **Efficient & Streamlined:** Minimize friction for common tasks, particularly for field officials and managers.
- **Guided & Validated:** Implement strict business rules to prevent scheduling conflicts and eligibility errors.

## Short-term Priorities
- Implement new features specifically aimed at further simplifying the day-to-day management of leagues and team schedules.
