What "Templates" button opens
Clicking 📚 Templates opens a full-screen modal overlay — the Template Library.
It's the single entry point for:
  • Browsing and applying tournament formats (replaces Generate Tournament modal)
  • Browsing and applying saved server-side templates
  • Saving the current schedule as a new template
  • Managing templates: rename, clone, delete, preview
